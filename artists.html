Samiya Mumtaz
Biography

Samiya Mumtaz is a critically acclaimed Pakistani film and television actress, known for her intense and nuanced portrayals of complex characters. Born in Karachi, known for choosing unconventional, socially relevant roles, she has starred in blockbuster dramas like Meri Zaat Zarra-e-Benishan, Sadqay Tumhare, and Udaari. Film debut in Dukhtar (2014). Passionate about organic farming and environmental activism.

Shows Participated In
  • Darya Kay Iss Paar
  • Chok Chakkar
  • Yaar Julahay
Zia Mohyeddin
Biography

Legendary intellectual, theatre artist, and broadcaster. Zia Mohyeddin (1931-2023) is known for his eloquent storytelling and theatrical expertise, directing and acting in Shakespearean classics and establishing NAPA and making him a giant in both Eastern and Western theatrical circles.

Shows Participated In
  • The Tempest
  • Othello
  • Jaza Saza
Anwar Maqsood
Biography

Legendary Pakistani scriptwriter, satirist, and theatre innovator, known for classics like Fifty Fifty, Aangan Terha, and Loose Talk, and for bringing acclaimed satirical plays to the stage. Recipient of Hilal-e-Imtiaz and Pride of Performance.

Shows Participated In
  • Sawa 14 August
  • Anwar Maqsood Ka Dharna
  • House Arrest
Naseeruddin Shah
Biography

Renowned Indian actor and theatre artist, acclaimed for his versatile performances on stage and screen, and for shaping modern Indian theatre with works like Mohan Joshi Hazir Ho! and numerous classical and contemporary plays.

Shows Participated In
  • Ismat Apa Ke Naam
  • Einstein
  • A walk In The Woods
Om Puri
Biography

Acclaimed Indian actor and theatre performer, known for his powerful presence on stage and screen, bridging Indian parallel cinema and international films with memorable performances in socially relevant and classical works.

Shows Participated In
  • Punjab Kala Manch (Patiala)
  • Ghashiram Kotwal
  • Teri Amrita
Ratna Pathak Shah
Biography

Veteran Indian actress and director, celebrated for her versatile performances on stage and screen, known for blending classical theatre training with contemporary and experimental roles.

Shows Participated In
  • Antigone
  • Don Juan in Hell
  • The Caine Mutiny Court-Martial
Ian Mackellen
Biography

Acclaimed English actor and stage legend, renowned for his powerful performances in Shakespearean theatre and modern plays, as well as for his iconic film roles.

Shows Participated In
  • The Promise
  • Hamlet
  • Acting Shakespeare
Judi Dench
Biography

Dame Judi Dench, born in York, England, is a legendary actress of stage and screen, celebrated for her Shakespearean roles and versatility across comedy and tragedy, with a career spanning over seven decades.

Shows Participated In
  • The Good Companions
  • Measure for Measure
  • Romeo and Juliet
Nicholas Hytner
Biography

Sir Nicholas Hytner, born in 1956, is an acclaimed English theatre and film director, former Artistic Director of London's National Theatre, and co-founder of the Bridge Theatre, celebrated for productions like War Horse and The History Boys.

Shows Participated In
  • Julius Caesar
  • Henry V
  • Miss Saigon
