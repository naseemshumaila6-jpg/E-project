Awami Theatre Festival

Annual multi-play theatre festival featuring ~30 performances in Urdu (and also Punjabi, Sindhi, Saraiki, Balochi, Memon). Includes a special open-air show Dukhtar-e-Kashmir on 6 Feb. Plays include titles like Seedhi Jaleebi, Mirza Ghalib in Karachi, Bohat Ho Gayi Begum, Yeh Kaisa Daur Hai?, Wah Tera Kya Kehna, Mujh Mein Tu Maujood, and more.

Jashan-e-Rekhta

Jashn-e-Rekhta Dubai is a premier Urdu festival celebrating poetry, music, literature, and art, bringing together artists and enthusiasts from around the world to experience the richness of Urdu culture.

Macbeth

Macbeth is a powerful Indian theatre production of Shakespeare’s classic tragedy, following a brave general’s descent into ambition, murder, guilt, and downfall as he seizes the throne and faces the consequences of his actions.

Park

Park is a Hindi comedy theatre play about three men humorously battling for space and benches in a public park, turning a simple situation into a witty and relatable commentary on territory and everyday life.
